Origin · Year Zero

The Founding Event

On a Tuesday morning in a research basement in Lagos, Dr Amara Okafor ran a test she wasn't supposed to run. The safety officer was on vacation. Nobody had approved the power draw. The resonance array had never been tested above 40% coherence.

She ran it at 97%.

What followed was not an explosion. It was a door. And on the other side of it — stars that had never been named, skies that had never been breathed under, worlds that had been waiting without knowing what they were waiting for.

Technology · The Lattice

Micro-wormholes exist in every cubic millimetre of space, billions of times per second.

Dr Okafor did not invent them. She learned to hold one open long enough to walk through. What she called the Lattice is simply the name for what was already there — a navigable network of spontaneous quantum geometry, scaled from the Planck length to the width of a doorway.

3' Lock Duration

The minimum time to synchronise two nodes. At stellar distances, longer. The universe makes you wait.

0.1c Bubble Ships

Alcubierre-pattern vessels. The only way to reach a world before a beacon is placed. Survey Corps lives here.

∞ Beacon Lifespan

Fusion-powered resonance beacons. One million year theoretical lifespan. Immutable signature burned at creation.

600 Worlds Mapped

Lattice-compatible. Unreachable at launch. The Survey Corps will spend thirty-five years unlocking them.

The Lattice finds beacons by signal, not coordinates. The signal travels with the beacon. The beacon travels with its star system. The galaxy moves at 630 km/s through the Local Group. In three minutes of lock time, the destination moves thousands of kilometres from where your charts said it was. Coordinates are useless. The signal is everything.

as the day it was placed. The signal never stops calling."

The Permanence Covenant

What we promise. What we cannot undo.

The Permanence Covenant is not a terms of service. It is a smart contract on Ethereum L2, a legal obligation, and the founding principle of everything built here. Certain promises cannot be revoked by a developer decision, a board vote, or a corporate acquisition.

  • Your dynasty's history exists in the Remembrance forever. No account closure deletes it.
  • KALON cannot be purchased with real money. Ever. By anyone.
  • The MARKS system lives on Ethereum L2. It does not belong to us.
  • The Remembrance is replicated to an independent foundation archive in real time.
  • If the studio closes, source code is Apache 2.0 within 30 days. Automatically.
  • No countdown timers on decisions. No timed exclusives. No FOMO mechanics. Ever.
  • No server wipe. No server merge. No manufactured reset. Not in Year 1, not in Year 35.
  • Gameplay cannot close your account. Only violation of our terms can — and only you.
  • The developer does not have a cheat code. We cannot give ourselves KALON, MARKS, or votes.

The studio closure provision executes automatically. A pre-funded escrow on L2 releases MARKS migration to public self-custody if we fail to trigger it within 30 days of declared closure. This does not require a human decision. It does not wait for our cooperation.

"The game is designed to still be running
when founding players' children are adults."
