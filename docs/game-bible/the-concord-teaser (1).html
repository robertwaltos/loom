I
The Zero Point Field

The Casimir effect measures the quantum vacuum's pressure directly — plates pushed together by zero point field energy since 1948. At the Planck scale, spacetime foams with micro-wormhole geometries collapsing billions of times per second. The technology does not create this. It catches one before it collapses.

II
ER = EPR

Maldacena and Susskind, 2013: entanglement between two particles is mathematically equivalent to a wormhole connecting them. Not metaphor. The same geometry described two ways. An entangled pair is a micro-wormhole. The Lattice is the applied consequence of that equivalence at macroscopic scale.

III
Frequency Lock

Two nodes synchronise oscillations across the zero point field. When synchronisation reaches critical coherence, the field achieves topological equivalence between them. Two points vibrating identically in the same medium are that instant the same point. The traveller resolves at the destination. Not sent. Not received. Simply there.

IV
No Time Dilation

Time dilation occurs because you move through spacetime. In a coherence transfer you do not move. There is no in-between state. Your watch reads the same at destination as at departure. Exactly like flying versus driving — a different relationship with terrain, not faster movement. Except there is no terrain. There is no between.

The field was always doing this.
We just learned to read it.

The Core Insight · Why The Lattice Is Permanent

The signal travels
with the source.

Every FTL concept ever proposed has a targeting problem almost never addressed because it is so uncomfortable. Everything in the universe is moving. Earth at 30 km/s. The Sun at 220 km/s. The galaxy at 630 km/s. There is no fixed point. Every coordinate calculated for a destination is already wrong by the time the calculation finishes.

The Solution

The buoy does not broadcast a location. It broadcasts a signal. The signal travels with the buoy. The buoy travels with the star system. The star system moves however the universe moves it. None of that matters. The Lattice does not target coordinates. It targets the signal. The handshake does not ask: where are you? It asks: are you there? The answer is always yes.

The Analogy

You do not need to know where your phone is on the planet to call it. You do not calculate GPS coordinates, account for Earth's rotation, adjust for orbital velocity. You dial a number. The number finds the phone. The Lattice works identically. The buoy's frequency is its number. The zero point field is the network. The universe can expand and fold for the next billion years. The buoy keeps broadcasting. Any node that knows the frequency can reach it. Location is irrelevant. Identity is everything.

Year 290 → Present · The Dark Implication
Frequency Spoofing

The most dangerous thing in The Concord is not a weapon. It is a frequency spoofer. A device that makes a node believe it has locked with one destination when it has achieved coherence with another. Worlds don't go dark because their buoys fail. They go dark because one day the handshake completes — but the person who steps through is somewhere else. Players have been noticing transit log discrepancies since Year 290. The Architect's calibration reports have been impeccable. The discrepancies remain unexplained.
