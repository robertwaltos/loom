Context · The World of 2031

Humanity woke up one morning
and found out it had won.

Zero-point energy reached commercial scale in 2024. By 2031, power was effectively free. When energy is free, water is free. When water is free, food is abundant. When food is abundant, the territorial anxiety that drove human conflict for two hundred millennia quietly dissolved.

Matterwave synthesis made element extraction obsolete. Nanite therapies extended healthy lifespans past what any previous generation thought possible. The great compression of human anxiety — scarcity, disease, death — began, for the first time, to relax.

And then Dr Amara Okafor ran a test in a Lagos basement. And the universe opened a door.

The Chronicle · Permanent · Append-Only · Yours

When you might live forever,
significance becomes the scarce resource.

The Chronicle is the answer to the question nobody in the Age of Radiance knows how to ask out loud: if I never have to die, how do I prove that I lived?

Every dynasty. Every decision. Every world opened. Every crisis survived. Every alliance forged. Every discovery made. Written in an append-only, cryptographically chained, foundation-replicated record that outlasts its authors — by design, by contract, and by code.

The Chronicle was built before you arrived, because we knew you were going to need it.

  • Every entry is permanent — append-only, SHA-256 chained, tamper-evident by mathematics
  • Real-time replication to an independent foundation archive on a separate cloud provider
  • Full-text searchable forever — a player in Year 105 can read what you wrote in Year 1
  • Two witnesses can write conflicting accounts of the same event — both are canonical. History holds both.
  • Dynasty Completion entries are final — the arc finished, the record is closed, the story stays
  • Studio closure provisions: source Apache 2.0, archive to foundation, automatic. Not a promise. Code.

"Your story is permanent. Build something worth reading."
