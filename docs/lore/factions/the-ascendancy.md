# The Ascendancy

**Type:** Antagonist Faction
**First Appearance:** Year 1 (frequency anomalies)
**Full Emergence:** Year 8 (integrated events, civilisational fracture risk)

## Overview

The Ascendancy is the primary antagonist force in The Concord. They operate through the Lattice itself, corrupting the infrastructure that connects worlds rather than confronting players directly. Their influence manifests as frequency anomalies in transit logs before escalating into coordinated lattice disruption.

## Mechanisms

- **Frequency Spoofing:** Corrupt beacon signals to redirect Silfen Weave travellers
- **Harmonic Interference:** Inject false harmonics into lattice node signatures
- **Beacon Degradation:** Systematically degrade resonance beacons from `active` through `compromised` to `destroyed`
- **Lattice Integrity Attacks:** Reduce world integrity scores, directly impacting KALON issuance via the Stellar Standard formula

## Threat Progression

| Year | Manifestation |
|------|--------------|
| 1-3 | Isolated frequency anomalies in transit logs |
| 4-7 | Coordinated beacon disruption, Void-Touched worlds emerge |
| 8+ | Integrated civilisation-scale events, fracture risk |

## Void-Touched Worlds

15 worlds (3% of the 600 total) carry Ascendancy influence. These Void-Touched worlds exhibit modified physics and serve as forward operating zones for Ascendancy activity.

## Player Interaction

- Detection of frequency spoofing anomalies in transit logs is a core discovery mechanic
- Beacon restoration requires coordinated multi-player effort
- Lattice integrity restoration is an economic imperative (degraded integrity = reduced KALON issuance)
- Investigation of Ascendancy patterns feeds into The Architect's Okafor Convergence research

## Design Principles

The Ascendancy is infrastructure-level threat, not a military one. They corrupt the connections between worlds, not the worlds themselves. Players fight them with investigation, coordination, and beacon maintenance — not combat.
