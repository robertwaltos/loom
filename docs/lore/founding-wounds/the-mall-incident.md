# The Mall Incident

**Type:** Founding Wound
**Duration:** 4 minutes 17 seconds (4:17)
**Coordinates:** 38.8895°N 77.0353°W

## Overview

The Mall Incident is The Concord's founding wound — the event from which the entire universe unfolds. In 4 minutes and 17 seconds, something happened at those coordinates that created the conditions for everything that followed.

## Known Facts

1. **Duration is canonical:** 4:17 is the sacred number. The Founding Mark window (43,200 blocks on L2) is mathematically scaled from this duration.
2. **The test ran:** Okafor's logs confirm that a safety officer was on vacation and a test proceeded without oversight. The nature of the test is never fully disclosed.
3. **Location is deliberate:** The coordinates point to Washington DC. The real-world resonance is intentional — The Concord's reality bleeds into ours at its inception.
4. **Precognitive data:** Okafor's 847 years of geodetic records pre-dating the event by centuries suggest either temporal anomaly or an observation window that transcends conventional time.

## In-World Impact

The Mall Incident is the reason the Lattice exists. The founding wound created the substrate that connects 600 worlds across the Silfen Weave. Every frequency signature, every beacon resonance, every transit lock carries an echo of those 4 minutes and 17 seconds.

## The Founding Mark

The economic connection: the Founding Mark window (43,200 L2 blocks) during which early players can establish their dynasties and receive founding KALON allocations is derived directly from the incident's duration. This makes the economy itself a monument to the founding wound.

## The Witness Protocol

Players participate in the founding through the Witness Protocol — submitting witness accounts and signatures before launch. These are validated and replayed during the founding event broadcast via Pixel Streaming, making every early player a co-author of the founding narrative.

## Narrative Arc

| Phase | Revelation |
|-------|-----------|
| Launch | The 4:17 duration is established. Players know something happened. |
| Years 1-3 | Okafor Log fragments surface. The coordinates and record-keeping emerge. |
| Years 4-7 | Players begin correlating Okafor data with Ascendancy emergence patterns. |
| Year 8+ | The Architect publishes the Okafor Convergence findings. The founding wound proves to be alive — still radiating through the Lattice. |

## Design Principles

The Mall Incident is never fully explained. It is the question the universe is built to explore, not answer. Each revelation should raise two new questions. The 4:17 duration should appear in unexpected places throughout the game — hidden in harmonic frequencies, lattice node distances, beacon timing intervals — as Easter eggs for attentive players.
