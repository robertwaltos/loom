# What Is The Chronicle?

> Player-facing explanation. New tone: luminous, invitational, forward.

## The Short Version

The Chronicle is the permanent record of everything that matters in The Concord.
Every dynasty founded, every alliance forged, every world settled, every crisis
survived — written into an append-only chain that cannot be altered, deleted, or
forgotten.

Your story is permanent. Build something worth reading.

## The Longer Version

When the Lattice opened and six hundred worlds became reachable, humanity faced a
question it had never needed to answer before: what do you do when scarcity ends?

Energy is free. Matter can be synthesised. Death is becoming optional. The old
drivers of history — hunger, territory, survival — are dissolving. In their place,
a new scarcity has emerged: significance.

The Chronicle is the answer. It is not a memorial for the dead. It is a record for
the living who want to matter. Every action you take in The Concord — every decision,
every alliance, every betrayal — is recorded permanently. Not because you might die
and be forgotten. Because you might live forever and never matter.

## How It Works

- **Append-only**: Entries are added. Never removed. Never edited. Never deleted.
- **Hash-chained**: Every entry's integrity is verified by the entry that follows it.
  Tamper with one entry and the entire chain breaks.
- **Foundation replicated**: The Chronicle is stored across multiple independent
  systems maintained by the Foundation. No single point of failure.
- **Publicly readable**: Your Chronicle is yours, but it is not private. Anyone can
  read what you have built. This is by design.

## The Active Voice

The Chronicle is not something that happens to you. You build it.

- You **write** your Chronicle entries through your actions
- You **build** your dynasty's record through your decisions
- You **earn** depth through the significance of your contributions

Every time you see "Build your Chronicle," remember: this is not preservation.
This is creation.
