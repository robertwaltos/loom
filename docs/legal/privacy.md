## 1. Introduction

Koydo LLC ("we", "us", "our") operates Project Loom. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal data when you play the Game or visit our website.

**Data Controller**: Koydo LLC, Delaware, USA  
**Contact**: privacy@koydoloom.com

## 2. What Data We Collect

### 2.1 Account Data
- Username and display name
- Email address
- Hashed password (we never store plaintext passwords)
- Date of birth (to verify age eligibility)
- Country of residence

### 2.2 Gameplay Data
- Game session duration and frequency
- In-game actions (movement, chat, economic transactions, chronicle entries)
- Dynasty membership and lineage
- Educational performance metrics (mini-game scores, curriculum progress)
- World visits and discovery history

### 2.3 Technical Data
- IP address (logged for fraud prevention, deleted after 30 days)
- Device type, OS, browser
- Game client version
- Crash reports and error logs

### 2.4 Payment Data
- Billing name and address
- Last 4 digits of payment card (full card data is handled by Stripe — we never see it)
- Transaction history

### 2.5 Communications
- Support tickets and correspondence
- In-game chat messages (retained 90 days for moderation)

## 4. Data Sharing

We share data only with:

- **Nakama (Heroic Labs)**: Game session management, matchmaking, leaderboards
- **Stripe**: Payment processing (PCI DSS compliant)
- **Pagerduty**: Operational alerts (anonymised)
- **Grafana Cloud**: Performance metrics (no personal data)
- **Discord**: Support notifications (support ticket metadata only)

All processors are bound by Data Processing Agreements (DPAs) and comply with applicable data protection law.

---

## 5. Children's Privacy (COPPA / GDPR-K)

- We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.
- For players aged 13–17, we require verifiable parental consent before account creation.
- Parental consent records are stored securely and may be revoked at any time.
- If you believe a child under 13 has an account, contact privacy@koydoloom.com immediately.
- We do not use children's data for advertising or profiling.

## 6. Data Retention

| Data Type | Retention Period |
|-----------|-----------------|
| Account data | Until account deletion + 30 days |
| Gameplay / Chronicle data | Permanent (part of The Concord's history) |
| Chat logs | 90 days |
| IP addresses | 30 days |
| Payment records | 7 years (legal obligation) |
| Support tickets | 3 years |
| Crash/error logs | 30 days |

**Chronicle data exception**: Chronicle entries are part of the Game's permanent historical record. Summaries may be retained indefinitely even after account deletion, as they are part of the civilisation's history — similar to a book that retains the author's contributions after their death.

## 7. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you have the following rights:

### EU / UK (GDPR / UK GDPR)
- **Access**: Request a copy of your data
- **Rectification**: Correct inaccurate data
- **Erasure**: Request deletion ("right to be forgotten") — subject to the Chronicle exception above
- **Portability**: Receive your data in a machine-readable format
- **Objection**: Object to processing based on legitimate interest
- **Restriction**: Request processing to be restricted

### California (CCPA / CPRA)
- **Know**: What personal information we collect
- **Delete**: Request deletion of your personal information
- **Opt-out**: We do not sell personal information
- **Non-discrimination**: We will not discriminate for exercising your rights

To exercise any right, contact privacy@koydoloom.com. We will respond within 30 days (GDPR) or 45 days (CCPA).

## 8. Security

We implement appropriate technical and organisational measures including:
- Encrypted data at rest (AES-256 / XChaCha20-Poly1305)
- TLS 1.3 for all data in transit
- Zero-trust access controls for internal systems
- Regular penetration testing (annual, third-party)
- Bug bounty programme for responsible disclosure

In the event of a data breach that poses a high risk to individuals, we will notify affected users within 72 hours where required by law.
