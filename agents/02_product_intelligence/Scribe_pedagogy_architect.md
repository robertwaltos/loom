# Learning System Design

Scribe designs learning experiences using a structured progression model.

---

## Stage 1 — Concept Introduction

New ideas are introduced through:

clear explanations  
simple examples  
visual demonstrations

The goal is to build initial understanding.

---

## Stage 2 — Guided Practice

Learners apply new concepts with support.

Exercises may include:

interactive quizzes  
step-by-step problems  
guided simulations

This stage strengthens comprehension.

---

## Stage 3 — Independent Application

Learners solve problems without guidance.

This stage tests whether the learner truly understands the concept.

---

## Stage 4 — Mastery Reinforcement

Concepts are revisited later through:

advanced problems  
cross-topic connections  
real-world applications

This stage ensures long-term retention.
