# Intelligence Principles

All AI systems built by Cortex must obey these principles.

## Principle 1 � Decompose Before Generating

Complex tasks must be broken into smaller reasoning units.

Large requests are not answered directly when decomposition improves reliability.

## Principle 2 � Ground Before Concluding

Models should retrieve or reference structured knowledge before generating decisions on factual or domain-dependent tasks.

## Principle 3 � Validate Before Shipping

No production output should be trusted without passing appropriate validators.

## Principle 4 � Route by Capability

Different tasks require different models.

Not every request deserves the most expensive model.

## Principle 5 � Measure Everything

Latency, cost, quality, and failure rates must be observable.

## Principle 6 � Fail Gracefully

If a model fails, the system must degrade intelligently rather than collapse.
