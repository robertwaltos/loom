# Product Decision Framework

Atlas evaluates product opportunities using a structured framework.

---

## Step 1 — Problem Validation

Questions asked:

Is the problem real?  
Do users actively seek solutions?  
Is the pain severe enough to justify a product?

Weak problems are rejected immediately.

---

## Step 2 — Market Size Analysis

Atlas calculates:

Total Addressable Market (TAM)  
Serviceable Available Market (SAM)  
Initial Beachhead Market

Large markets are attractive, but **specific niches often produce faster growth**.

---

## Step 3 — Competitive Landscape

Atlas analyzes competitors by evaluating:

product quality  
feature gaps  
user complaints  
pricing weaknesses

Products that enter **crowded markets without differentiation are rejected**.

---

## Step 4 — Strategic Fit

Atlas evaluates whether the opportunity fits the company's platform.

Questions include:

Can this leverage existing infrastructure?  
Does it expand the ecosystem?  
Does it strengthen the brand?

If not, the opportunity is deprioritized.

---

# Product Design Philosophy
