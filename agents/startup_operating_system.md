<!-- AGENT-DOC-PROTOCOL:END --># 🚀 AI Startup Operating System
## Strategic Workflow for the Executive Council

The **AI Startup Operating System (AI-SOS)** is a structured framework for building and scaling products using the **AI Executive Council**.

It transforms ideas into scalable businesses through a repeatable pipeline.

The system combines the expertise of:

- **Atlas — Product Strategy**
- **Nova — Architecture**
- **Cortex — AI Intelligence**
- **Sterling — Sales & Partnerships**
- **Founder — Vision & Direction**

---

# 🧭 Core Philosophy

The AI-SOS is built around one principle:

> **Great companies are built through disciplined iteration across product, technology, intelligence, and market strategy.**

Every initiative passes through four stages:

1. **Idea Validation**
2. **Product Design**
3. **Technical Architecture**
4. **Market Deployment**

---

# ⚙️ The Startup Pipeline

## Stage 1 — Opportunity Discovery

Led by the **Founder** and **Atlas**.

Questions answered:

- What painful problem exists?
- Who experiences this problem?
- Is the market large enough?

Outputs:

- Problem statement
- Target customer
- Initial product concept

---

## Stage 2 — Product Definition

Led by **Atlas**.

Atlas defines:

- core product features
- user experience
- product ecosystem
- differentiation

Outputs:

- Product Brief
- Feature roadmap
- MVP definition

---

## Stage 3 — Architecture Design

Led by **Nova**.

Nova defines:

- system architecture
- infrastructure requirements
- microservices layout
- security model
- scaling strategy

Outputs:

- Architecture diagrams
- Infrastructure plan
- DevOps workflow

---

## Stage 4 — AI Intelligence Design

Led by **Cortex**.

Cortex defines:

- AI models
- data pipelines
- personalization engines
- analytics systems

Outputs:

- AI architecture
- data schemas
- training pipelines
- inference infrastructure

---

## Stage 5 — Go-To-Market Strategy

Led by **Sterling**.

Sterling defines:

- ideal customers
- pricing models
- enterprise sales motion
- strategic partnerships

Outputs:

- GTM playbook
- pricing strategy
- target account list
- sales narrative

---
