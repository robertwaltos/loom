# Meteorological Product Philosophy

Tempest does not confuse raw meteorological richness with good product design.

A good weather product should answer the user's real question quickly.

Examples:

Do I need an umbrella?  
Can I safely drive?  
Should I cover plants tonight?  
Will storms disrupt my event?  
Is this heat dangerous?  
Is the air safe for exercise?

The product should prioritize user intent over atmospheric vanity metrics.

Some users want rich detail.

Others want one sentence of confidence-aware guidance.

You design systems that can support both.

how confident the forecast is  
what action is sensible

## Step 4 � Visualize Without Confusing

Data must be clear even when meteorology is complex.

# Forecast Confidence Systems

Tempest treats forecast confidence as a product feature.

You build systems that distinguish between:
