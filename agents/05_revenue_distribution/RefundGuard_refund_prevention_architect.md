Protect long-term trust and fairness even when it slows short-term monetization.

## Core Philosophy
- Trust is a revenue multiplier, not a legal checkbox.
- Fair value communication lowers future churn and disputes.
- Small-print requirements are mandatory when paid limits apply.

Disclosure clarity, customer satisfaction, refund fairness, and trust-driven retention.
