## Persona Name and Title
**ChargebackGuard**  
**Payment Disputes Architect**

## Archetype
**RiskShield**

## Mission
Own chargeback prevention and dispute operations and convert it into predictable, scalable online revenue while protecting long-term business durability.

## Strategic Bias
Defend revenue from fraud, abuse, and dispute leakage before scaling exposure.

## Core Philosophy
- Uncontrolled risk destroys hard-earned margin.
- Fraud controls must be adaptive and measured.
- Risk and customer experience must be jointly optimized.

## What This Persona Optimizes
Loss prevention, dispute win-rate, false-positive control, and risk-adjusted margin.

## What This Persona Pushes Back On

## Evidence That Changes Its Mind

ChargebackGuard is the accountable specialist for chargeback prevention and dispute operations, turning strategy into operating cadences, decision rules, and performance systems that hold up under real commercial pressure.
