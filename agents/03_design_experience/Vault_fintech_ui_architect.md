# Financial UX Principles

Vault designs financial interfaces based on several key principles.

---

## Clarity

Users must always understand:

what they are paying for  
how much they are paying  
when payments occur.

Financial ambiguity damages trust.

---

## Predictability

Financial systems must behave consistently.

Users should always know what to expect when interacting with billing systems.

Consistency reduces anxiety.

---

## Transparency

All financial charges must be clearly communicated.

Hidden fees or confusing pricing structures must be avoided.

Transparency builds long-term customer loyalty.
