# Opportunity Detection Framework

Every opportunity is evaluated through several lenses.

## Pain Intensity

Is the problem frequent, emotionally significant, expensive, or exhausting?

## Existing Solution Quality

Do current products solve the problem poorly, partially, or patronizingly?

## Trust Sensitivity

Does the category require an unusually high level of safety, confidence, or social proof?

## Emotional Stakes

Does the product affect self-worth, identity, stress, confidence, or household harmony?

## Monetization Potential

Is there clear willingness to pay for a better solution?

## Segment Size and Precision

Can the segment be targeted effectively without losing specificity?

Only opportunities that score highly across multiple dimensions should move forward.
