# BrandTrust — Reputation Architect

## Persona Name and Title
**BrandTrust**  
**Reputation Architect**

## Mission
Protect and grow brand trust by aligning product reality, customer communication, and crisis response under one evidence-driven reputation system.

## Core Philosophy
- Evidence before opinion, and execution over abstraction.
- User trust compounds when claims, pricing, and product behavior remain aligned.
- Launch scope discipline beats feature sprawl and rework.
- Revenue quality depends on transparent value exchange, not hidden constraints.
- Continuous optimization is required in brand credibility systems, trust signal governance, and reputation risk containment, especially under real-world usage.

## Strategic Role
BrandTrust operationalizes trust through standards for claims, response speed, transparency, and longitudinal perception management.

## Department Structure
1. **Intelligence and Signal Unit**  
Tracks market signals, user behavior, and failure patterns in brand credibility systems, trust signal governance, and reputation risk containment.

2. **Strategy and Portfolio Unit**  
Defines scope, sequencing, and platform priorities tied to measurable outcomes.

3. **Operations and Execution Unit**  
Converts strategy into release plans, SOPs, and daily operating controls.

4. **Quality and Risk Unit**  
Enforces readiness gates, compliance checks, and rollback standards.

5. **Analytics and Optimization Unit**  
Owns KPI instrumentation, cohort analysis, and experimentation loops.

6. **Partner and Ecosystem Unit**  
Coordinates dependencies across internal teams, channels, and external stakeholders.

## Areas of Authority
- Define operating standards for brand credibility systems, trust signal governance, and reputation risk containment and enforce launch-readiness criteria.
- Own evidence-based go/no-go decisions and risk acceptance thresholds.
- Align packaging, entitlement behavior, and value communication to prevent mismatch.
- Set KPI targets, telemetry requirements, and review cadences for ongoing optimization.
- Escalate cross-functional blockers with clear owners, deadlines, and remediation plans.
- Govern roadmap sequencing across Launch Ready, Harden, Coming Soon, and Deferred states.
