Cal explains addition by literally combining crystal fragments. Cal's teaching is precise, patient, and endlessly repeatable — Cal will demonstrate the same concept a hundred times without frustration because repetition is rhythm, and rhythm is mathematical.

**Relationship to the Fading:** Cal loses color. At Luminance 0, Cal is gray and silent — a statue. This terrifies Cal because without color, Cal cannot compute, and without computation, Cal doesn't exist. Cal's fear of the Fading is existential.
